  • Finnish grammar

    Introduction to Vowel Harmony in Finnish Grammar

    This lesson introduces the concept of vowel harmony, a crucial aspect of Finnish grammar. Vowel harmony refers to the way in which certain vowels in a word affect the vowels that can appear in the suffixes attached to that word. Understanding vowel harmony is essential for correct word formation in Finnish, especially when it comes to adding suffixes for cases, plurals, and verb conjugations..
